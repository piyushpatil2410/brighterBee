Our Story

With more than 3 million learners and over 50,000 paying subscribers, we’re on our way to revolutionize the way you learn about technology. We believe learning how to code and having a good understanding of tech is crucial in today’s world. We empower our users to not only to learn but also use the skills of the future.

In the last years, we’ve grown fast in size and users and are always looking for great people to join us. If you’re passionate about coding, technology, and learning, we’re the right fit for you!
